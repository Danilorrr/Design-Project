Some Information About Football


Gridiron football (American Football) is a sport that has evolved from soccer (European football)
and rugby. There are 11 players on each side of the ball (offense and defense). There are 32 teams
in the NFL (National Football League), two of these teams make it to the Super Bowl
and compete against each other. This sport is very popular in the USA and Canada.
Hopefully I can get you to like this sport too.
